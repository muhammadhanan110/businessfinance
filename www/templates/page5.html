Horizontal Analysis

Formula

Horizontal and vertical analysis both are fundamental ratios of financial managment. Horizontal analysis in which comprision of current year with base year.

Horizontal analysis = New year item – old item / old year item

Vertical Analysis

Formula

Vertical is a method of income statement analysis in which each entry for each of the three major categories of accounts or assets liabilities and equities in a balance sheet is represented as a proportion of the total account it’s a comparison with same year.

Vertical analysis = item / sales X 100 Vertical analysis = items/ T. Assets X 100
